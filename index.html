Spotifry My Brain

If your brain is too fried to find new music, or maybe not fried enough (skip the acid), we’ve created an app that will help you discover new artists. Enter one of your favorite musicians and three related artists will pop up. You can click the artist name to be directed to a youtube video that will play automatically. Or, you can click on the least related artist image to display that artist’s three related artists, and so forth.
